About Us

“ We work upon framing precious memories that matters to you ”

Welcome to Studio Supersonic. A team of experienced and young enthusiast, based in Ranchi, possessing national destination wedding photography and cinematography professionals available to wrap esteemed weddings and events. The way we operate is natural, cheerful and productive, concentrating on impromptu moments and capturing real emotions. Supersonic offers creative and candid style of photography, along with beautiful enduring Photo Albums. For us, it’s the quality which is foremost, as we understand, the importance of your every special moment.

About Founder

Mr. Rajnandan Prasad the man behind the show has been associated with photography since 1990, has skilled himself in different forms of photography. With his learning aptitude, He has always tried to bring the advanced level of photography for his clients. Under his vision, team got the inspiration to provide Dedicated Services. The mission of studio supersonic is to combine technical proficiency with aesthetic intuition to produce rich, beautiful and effective photographs and videos.
